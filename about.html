Exceptional service at affordable prices.

Our company has proudly been serving the Connecticut shoreline for over 15 years. We've learned that carefully listening to our clients is the best way to ensure that we understand their needs so that they are thoroughly satisfied. We take great pride that our customer base is largely referrals. Many of our customers have been with us from the beginning!

Though mowing is a rather simple task, there are skills and equipment that enhance the results. Our objective is to provide our customers with pristine landscape maintenance at affordable costs. In order to keep our prices down, we have learned from experience that it is important to have partnerships with local providers. Thus, over the years we have developed partnerships with local businesses for most of the materials we use, making our materials easy to obtain and keeping our costs low.
